Pink Oyster Mushroom

Pleurotus djamor

Vibrant, fast-growing, and full of flair. Pink Oyster mushrooms turn heads with their stunning coral color and grow rapidly into beautiful clusters. Their mild, slightly seafood-like flavor becomes rich and nutty when cooked. Great sautéed or tossed into tacos and pastas. They’re fun to grow, beginner-friendly, and make an eye-catching kitchen centerpiece. Expect your first flush in under 10 days!

Shimeji Mushroom

Hypsizygus tessellatus

Delicate, nutty, and restaurant-worthy. Shimeji mushrooms are a gourmet favorite in Japanese cuisine, known for their elegant stems and subtly sweet, nutty taste. Excellent in soups, noodles, or stir-fried dishes, they offer a lovely crunch and hold their shape well during cooking. Our kit makes it easy to cultivate these culinary gems at home — even if you’ve never grown mushrooms before. Ready to harvest in 2–3 weeks.

Shiitake Mushroom

Lentinula edodes

Rich, savory, and loaded with umami. Shiitake mushrooms are prized in Asian cuisine for their meaty texture and deep, smoky flavor. With this kit, you can grow your own shiitake at home — perfect for stir-fries, soups, or grilled dishes. These mushrooms aren’t just tasty — they’re also known for their immune-boosting properties and are a powerhouse of B vitamins. Ready to harvest in just a few weeks!
